Symbol Glossary

A quick reference for every geometric symbol used in your numerology system.

  • Vertical Line: will, direction, identity
  • Horizontal Line: stability, rest, foundation
  • Circle: soul, unity, wholeness
  • Triangle: action, fire, manifestation
  • Square: structure, discipline, order
  • Spiral: growth, evolution, creativity
  • Infinity Loop: cycles, power, flow
  • X Shape: crossroads, choices, change
  • V Shape: path, direction, movement
