Sacred Geometry Meditation

Visualizing your number’s shape helps you align with its energy. These meditations are simple but powerful.

Breath + Shape Method

Inhale while tracing the shape in your mind. Exhale while imagining it glowing brighter. Repeat 9 times.

Shape Expansion

Visualize your shape expanding outward from your chest, filling your aura with its meaning and strength.

Shape Integration

Imagine your shape rotating slowly, then merging into your body. This helps integrate the number’s lessons.
