Numbers 1–9 as Sacred Geometry

Each numerology number expresses a specific geometric vibration. These shapes help you visualize your strengths, challenges, and life themes.

  • 1 — Pillar: vertical line + base triangle (will, direction)
  • 2 — Dual Arc: mirrored curves (duality, intuition)
  • 3 — Spiral Flame: spiral + flick (creativity, expression)
  • 4 — Square Gate: open square (structure, discipline)
  • 5 — Crossroads: X + open V (change, freedom)
  • 6 — Cradle: circle + downward line (care, responsibility)
  • 7 — Blade: horizontal + diagonal cut (analysis, truth)
  • 8 — Infinity Tower: infinity loop + vertical line (power, cycles)
  • 9 — Ascension Flame: circle + upward triangle (completion, wisdom)
