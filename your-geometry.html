Your Personal Sacred Geometry

Every person has a unique geometric signature based on their numerology chart. These shapes represent your core energies and how they interact.

Life Path Geometry

Your Life Path number determines your primary geometric form. This shape represents your long-term lessons, natural tendencies, and the energy you walk with throughout life.

Expression/Destiny Geometry

This shape shows how you build, create, and express yourself in the world. It often complements or challenges your Life Path geometry.

Soul Urge Geometry

This is your inner shape—the geometry of your heart, desires, and emotional truth. It reveals what you need to feel fulfilled.

Personality Geometry

This shape represents how others see you. It’s the outer layer of your energetic structure.
